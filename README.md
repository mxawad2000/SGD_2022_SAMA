We use short case study of UAEU students to show The paper presents survey results before and after using the proposed mobile application on sustainability. We present an awareness solution to educate and support SDGs among college students. We analysis of survey shows that awareness has improved among IT students in the UAE University. Survey results indicate that 49% of participants have no idea what the 17 goals are, which is a large proportion. User experience of SAMA showed that 97.8% of participants are willing to contribute to achieve one or more SDG goals, and 90% would like to be updated with the latest UAE contributions on achieving SDGs.
